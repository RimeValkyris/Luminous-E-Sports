PLAY BEYOND LIMITS

Luminous E-Sports is a rising force in competitive gaming, built on passion, skill, and teamwork. With a commitment to excellence, they continue to shine on the big stage and inspire the next generation of players.

OUR HISTORY

From humble beginnings to dominating the digital arena, here’s how our journey in esports unfolded.

2021 - The Beginning

Our e-sports journey started with a small group of passionate gamers who shared one dream: to make a mark in competitive gaming.

2022 - First tournament

We joined our very first official esports tournament, gaining experience, exposure, and building a name in the community.

2023 - Expansion & Partnerships

We expanded into new game titles and partnered with local brands to further strengthen our presence in the e-sports industry.

2024 - National Recognition

Our team reached the national stage, competing against the best and showcasing our skills to a larger audience.

2025 - Continuing the Legacy

Today, we stand as one of the most promising esports organizations, inspiring the next generation of gamers and fans alike.
